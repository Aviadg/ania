❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any specific English level to start?

We recommend at least B1 level (intermediate) to get the most out of the course. However, we provide extra support materials for those who need it. Take our level test above to check if you're ready!

How much time do I need to dedicate per week?

We recommend 4-6 hours per week: watching videos (2-3 hours), practicing exercises (1-2 hours), and reviewing materials (1 hour). The course is designed to fit into a busy professional schedule.

What if I can't attend the live sessions?

All live sessions are recorded and available for you to watch later. We also offer flexible scheduling for 1-on-1 sessions to accommodate different time zones and busy schedules.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the course content after the first month, we'll refund your payment in full.

Will I get a certificate after completion?

Absolutely! Upon successful completion of all modules and assignments, you'll receive a personalized certificate that you can add to your professional credentials and LinkedIn profile.
